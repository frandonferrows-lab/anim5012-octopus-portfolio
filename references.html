Evidence Notes

Some husbandry recommendations for the Giant Pacific Octopus are supported by direct species-specific guidance, especially the AZA and BIAZA husbandry manuals. However, published evidence is still limited in some areas, so recommendations on nutrition, enclosure design and preventative monitoring should be interpreted alongside professional aquarium experience and veterinary judgement.

Where exact numerical requirements are not available, this portfolio uses cautious, evidence-led recommendations based on natural history, public aquarium practice and closely related cephalopod husbandry principles.
